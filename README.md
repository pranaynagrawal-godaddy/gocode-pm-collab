# GoCode PM Cohort Wall

This is the Week 3 exercise for the GoCode PM Accelerator. Your job is to add your card to this page — without writing a single line of code.

**Live page:** https://pranaynagrawal-godaddy.github.io/gocode-pm-collab/

---

## What you're doing

Every card on that page was added by a PM going through this exact exercise. When you submit your change and it gets approved, your card appears on the live site automatically — no deployment needed on your end.

---

## How to add your card

**Step 1 — Fork this repo**
Click the **Fork** button at the top right of this page. This creates your own personal copy of the repo that you can freely edit.

**Step 2 — Clone your fork**
On your fork's page, click the green **Code** button and copy the URL. Then open Claude Code and say:
> *"Help me clone this repo locally: [paste your fork URL]"*

**Step 3 — Create a branch**
Tell Claude:
> *"Create a branch for my name"*

**Step 4 — Add your card**
Tell Claude:
> *"Add me to the cohort page — my name is [your name], my title is [your title]"*

**Step 5 — Commit and push**
Tell Claude:
> *"Commit and push my changes"*

**Step 6 — Open a pull request**
Go to your fork on GitHub. You'll see a yellow banner — click **Compare & pull request** and submit. You're done. The facilitator will review and merge it.

Once merged, your card goes live on the cohort page within 30 seconds.
